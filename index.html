Environmental Database and Mapping

Due to the high impact that zoonotic pathogen's have on West Africa, spatio-temporal modeling of zoonotic pathogens is critical for understanding how the risk of pathogen infection varies across space. To help aid in the modeling effort, we have developed a database that contains environmental raster data that can be incorporated into disease modeling. Our data collection is unique in that we fetch data from various sources, crop that data to the boundaries of West Africa, and allow for easy download of the raster data.
